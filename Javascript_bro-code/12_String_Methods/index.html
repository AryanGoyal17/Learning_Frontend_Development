String Methods

        String Methods: (allow you to manipulate and work with text(strings))

        1-- .charAt(index of character you want)
        2-- .indexOf(character or string) [returns index of first occurence]
        3-- .lastIndexOf(character or string) [returns index of last occurence]
        4-- .length [returns length of string] 
        5-- .trim() [removes whitespaces before or after the string]
        6-- .toUpperCase(), .toLowerCase() [convert all characters uppercase or lowercase]
        7-- .repeat(No of times you want to repeat the string)
        8-- .startsWith(character/string you are checking for), .endsWith(character/string you are checking for) [returns a boolean]
        9-- .includes(character/string you are checking for) [returns a boolean]
        10-- .replaceAll (character you are replacing, character you are replacing with)
        11-- .padStart(), .padEnd() [Look at the js examples to understand this]
